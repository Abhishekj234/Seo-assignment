SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is a digital marketing strategy focused on optimizing a website's content, structure, and technical elements to improve its visibility and ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). HTML, which stands for Hypertext Markup Language, is a fundamental language used to structure and present content on the web. Importance of SEO:
Increased Visibility: SEO helps your website rank higher in search engine results. When your site appears on the first page or near the top of search results, it's more likely to be seen by users. Most people don't go beyond the first few search results pages, so high rankings are crucial for visibility.

Quality Traffic: SEO targets users who are actively searching for information, products, or services related to your website's content. This means the traffic generated through SEO is often more qualified and likely to convert into customers, subscribers, or leads.

Credibility and Trust: Websites that rank highly in search results are often perceived as more trustworthy and credible by users. People tend to trust search engines, so appearing at the top can boost your website's authority and reputation.

Cost-Effective: Compared to paid advertising, SEO is a cost-effective way to attract traffic to your website over the long term. While it requires an initial investment in terms of time and resources, the ongoing maintenance is generally more affordable than continuous ad spending.

Better User Experience: SEO isn't just about optimizing for search engines; it also involves improving the user experience on your website. This includes making your site faster, mobile-friendly, and easier to navigate. A better user experience can lead to higher engagement and conversion rates.
